ROMANCE : UNTOLD -daydream-

K-pop powerhouse ENHYPEN expand fantastical exploration through depths of ‘ROMANCE’ with 2nd Studio Repackage Album ROMANCE : UNTOLD -daydream- Expanding upon themes from ENHYPEN’s 2nd Studio Album ROMANCE : UNTOLD, the repackage edition, ROMANCE : UNTOLD -daydream- draws listeners into a vivid vision of a romantic and yet oddly spectral daydream. The septet expresses their love and appreciation for their fans ENGENE through the album, bringing the listeners through a daydream in which one growingly becomes certain of their feelings of love in the long hours of the day spent without the ‘other.’ Since their debut, ENHYPEN have solidified their identity by introducing unique concepts—such as surreal, dreamy, and youthful vibes—in presenting their own music and stories. The newly unveiled layer of the ‘ROMANCE’ series opens with an urban hip-hop track, “Daydream.” The track sings the heart of a boy who, in the absence of their lover, is pulled into a nightmare of one’s own imaginations and loneliness, but paradoxically falls even deeper in love. What follows is the danceable synth-pop track and lead single “No Doubt,” in which the boy becomes unequivocally sure about their love for the other, even in the face of challenges arising from their vastly different worlds, and even when circumstances do not allow them to spend every second together. The two new tracks complement the full tracklist from ENHYPEN’s previous release ROMANCE : UNTOLD, demonstrating the septet’s refined artistry in bringing fantastical concepts and deepened emotions to life based on their musical spectrum.
