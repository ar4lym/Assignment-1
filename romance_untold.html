Romance : Untold

Marking the beginning of the new ‘ROMANCE’ series, ENHYPEN are back with their 2nd Studio Album ROMANCE : UNTOLD. ROMANCE: UNTOLD narrates the fantastical romance story of seven boys who fall in love with ‘you,’ despite coming from vastly different worlds. Although the boys harbor many secrets, ‘you’ accepts them as they are, and together, they build a relationship while keeping their identities hidden from the world. ‘You’ represents a special presence who makes the boys better individuals, and the boys are determined to protect this love at all costs, only if ‘you’ say yes. So far, ENHYPEN have established an unparalleled creative presence by channeling their emotions through their album narratives. ROMANCE: UNTOLD continues this journey, with every song in the album revolving intricately around the theme of boys pledging their loyalty to ‘you.’ It also demonstrates ENHYPEN’s evolved musical prowess, highlighted by the contributions of all seven members to the songwriting. The lead single “XO (Only If You Say Yes)” perfectly encapsulates this album’s narrative, expressing the boys’ commitment to fulfill your wishes to the fullest extent of their powers, but only if you allow them to. Complementing the lead single are versatile musical offerings from each B-side track—“Moonstruck,” which portrays a magical moonlit encounter, “Your Eyes Only,” a tale of secret love, “Hundred Broken Hearts,” determination to avoid a heartbreak, “Brought the Heat Back,” exploration of newfound jealousy, “Paranormal,” belief of the power of love, “Royalty,” a pledge for loyalty, and “Highway 1009,” metaphor for the journey forward to an endless highway—all interweaving to fulfill the album’s overarching narrative.
