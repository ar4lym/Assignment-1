Orange Blood

K-pop powerhouse ENHYPEN are back with 5th Mini Album ‘ORANGE BLOOD’ Since their debut album, ENHYPEN have conveyed their innermost stories through their unique narrative and concepts. The ‘BLOOD’ series unfolded a new narrative, which introduced the seven boys determined to make sacrifices for the blood-bound ‘you.’ Now, the pioneering storytellers are set to continue their journey and invite the global listeners to join their fantastical universe of ‘ORANGE BLOOD.’ ‘ORANGE BLOOD’ carries on the narrative initiated in ‘DARK BLOOD,’ wherein the boys, having once forgotten ‘you’ due to their ignorance, experience a profound awakening and pledge to sacrifice themselves for ‘you.’ Upon losing their power that once seemed eternal, the boys come to a profound awakening that nothing lasts forever. With this newfound realization, the boys wholeheartedly commit to loving ‘you’ as if there’s no tomorrow, just like the saying ‘Carpe diem.’ Through the warm and comforting radiance of orange light, ‘ORANGE BLOOD’ aims to convey the boys’ unwavering determination. By interweaving their personal narrative with that of the album and ‘DARK MOON: THE BLOOD ALTAR,’ the seven-piece act expands their artistry beyond music, laying the foundation for the next form of entertainment. The album features a lineup of seven genre-spanning tracks: “Mortal,” “Sweet Venom,” “Still Monster,” “Blind,” and “Orange Flower (You Complete Me).” The lead single “Sweet Venom” serves as a continuation of “Bite Me,” the lead single of ‘DARK BLOOD.’ After proving their love through sacrifice and coming to terms with the finite and mortal nature of the world, the boys pledge to remain by your side even as poison courses through their veins. Masterfully crafted with ENHYPEN’s distinctive concept and high-quality music, ‘ORANGE BLOOD’ is poised to be the album that resonates with listeners worldwide.
